Campus Life

School Garden

Was established in 2003

Saint Francis Xavier College is a Catholic high school, which invites all to come and learn, as well as having fun. It is a great school to go to if looking to get into good colleges.



School Library

Was made in 2010

Saint Francis Xavier Library is a good place to hangout with your friends or sit and read a book. You can go there before school or after school if looking for a calm mindset and getting taken care of before school and after school.



School Hall

Was made in 2004

The Saint Francis Xavier College Hall, is a good place to perform an annual drama performance, or having a whole college meeting, as well as doing some talent shows or even having a prayer or Mass.

School Achievements

School Athletics
Our school's athletes made it to Nationals

Saint Francis Xavier College has had many wins in athletics, with some of their students making it to the highest of levels, including nationals! The School continues on to go to extreme levels in their athletics, as they only include the best of the best. If you go to this school, make sure you try your best for the athletics selection, because you never know, you could be in the next Athletics and make it to the highest levels there ever has been.

What's New

  • New Buildings

    There has been an addition of buildings in our school. It has added a few new buildings such as the 'Gallagher Rooms' which is one of the school houses and in those rooms, learning can be more flexible and fun as they have a lot of space in the inside and in those classes that will be held their will be the classes of those who are advanced in certain subjects.
